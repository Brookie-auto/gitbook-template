# FAQ

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I update to the latest version?

Enable auto-update in Settings, or download the latest version from your dashboard.

### My task is stuck. What should I do?

1. Stop the task
2. Check your proxy connection
3. Verify your account is still logged in
4. Restart the task

### Can I run tasks on multiple devices?

Each license is tied to one device. Contact support to transfer your license.

### Where can I get help?

Open a ticket in our Discord server for direct support from the team.
