# Profiles

## Managing Your Profiles

Profiles store your billing and shipping information for quick checkout.

### Creating a Profile

1. Go to the **Profiles** tab
2. Click **New Profile**
3. Fill in the required fields:
   * **Profile Name**
   * **Billing Address**
   * **Shipping Address**
   * **Payment Info**
4. Click **Save Profile**

{% hint style="info" %}
Check "Same as Billing" to auto-fill shipping with your billing address.
{% endhint %}
