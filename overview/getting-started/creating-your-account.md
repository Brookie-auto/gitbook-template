# Creating Your Account

## Sign Up for TicketsPH

Getting started is quick and easy.

### Step 1: Visit the Registration Page

Click **"Get Started"** on the homepage or navigate to the registration page directly.

### Step 2: Fill In Your Details

Enter the following:

* **Full Name**
* **Email Address**
* **Password**

### Step 3: Log In

After registering, log in with your email and password. You'll be redirected to your personal dashboard.

{% hint style="success" %}
That's it! You're ready to start browsing events and securing tickets.
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="warning" %}
Make sure to use a valid email address — this is how we'll communicate booking updates to you.
{% endhint %}
