# Securing Tickets

## How to Book Your Tickets

### Step 1: Choose Your Event

Navigate to **Concerts & Events** and find the event you want to attend.

### Step 2: Click "Secure Ticket"

Click the **"Secure Ticket"** button on the event page. You must be logged in to proceed.

### Step 3: Select Your Preferences

* Choose your preferred **section** from the seat map
* Enter the **quantity** of tickets you need
* Add any **notes** for our team

### Step 4: Submit Your Booking

Click **"Confirm"** to submit your booking request. Our team will begin working on securing your tickets.

### Step 5: Track Your Booking

Head to **My Bookings** in your dashboard to track the status of your request.

| Status    | Meaning                                      |
| --------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| PENDING   | Our team is working on securing your tickets |
| PAID      | Tickets confirmed and payment processed      |
| CANCELLED | Booking was cancelled                        |

{% hint style="warning" %}
You can have a maximum of **3 active bookings** at any time. Complete or cancel existing bookings to make room for new ones.
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="info" %}
For most events, we confirm within **24–48 hours** of the selling date.
{% endhint %}
