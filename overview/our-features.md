# ✨ Our Features

## What Makes TicketsPH Stand Out

### 🎫 Premium Ticket Assistance

We handle the entire ticket procurement process for you. Simply browse events, submit a booking request, and our team takes care of the rest.

### 🔒 Secure & Trusted

Industry-standard security practices protect your information. Your payment details are encrypted, and you only pay once tickets are confirmed.

### ⚡ Fast Booking

For most events, we confirm tickets within 24–48 hours of the selling date. High-demand events may take slightly longer.

### 🎵 Multi-Platform Support

We currently support ticket procurement from **SM Tickets** and **Ticketnet**, covering the majority of major concerts and events in the Philippines.

### 📊 Personal Dashboard

Track all your bookings in real-time from your personal dashboard. View statuses, manage up to 3 active bookings, and stay updated on your tickets.

### 🗺️ Interactive Seat Maps

View venue layouts and select your preferred sections with our interactive seat map feature. See pricing and availability at a glance.

### 💰 Money-Back Guarantee

If we're unable to secure your tickets, you'll receive a full refund. No risk, no hassle.
