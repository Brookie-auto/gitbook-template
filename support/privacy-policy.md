# Privacy Policy

## TicketsPH Privacy Policy

_Last updated: March 2026_

### Information We Collect

* **Account information** — Name, email address, and password (encrypted)
* **Booking information** — Event preferences, section choices, and quantity
* **Usage data** — How you interact with our platform

### How We Use Your Information

* To process and manage your ticket bookings
* To communicate booking status updates
* To improve our platform and services
* To provide customer support

### Data Security

* Passwords are hashed using industry-standard encryption (bcrypt)
* All data is transmitted over secure HTTPS connections
* Payment information is encrypted and never stored in plain text

### Data Sharing

We do **not** sell or share your personal information with third parties, except:

* When required to process your ticket booking (e.g., providing delivery details)
* When required by law

### Your Rights

* **Access** — Request a copy of your personal data
* **Correction** — Update inaccurate information
* **Deletion** — Request deletion of your account and data

### Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at **support@ticketsph.com**.
