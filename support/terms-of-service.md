# Terms of Service

## TicketsPH Terms of Service

_Last updated: March 2026_

### 1. Service Description

TicketsPH is a premium ticket assistance platform that helps users secure tickets to concerts and events in the Philippines. We act as an intermediary service, procuring tickets on behalf of our users.

### 2. User Accounts

* You must provide accurate information when registering
* You are responsible for maintaining the security of your account
* One account per person

### 3. Booking Policy

* Users may have a maximum of 3 active bookings at any time
* Bookings are processed on a first-come, first-served basis
* Ticket availability is not guaranteed until status is marked as PAID

### 4. Payment & Refunds

* Payment is only required once tickets have been confirmed
* If we are unable to secure your tickets, a full refund will be issued
* Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days

### 5. Cancellation Policy

* Pending bookings may be cancelled at any time
* Paid bookings are subject to the event organizer's cancellation policy

### 6. Limitation of Liability

TicketsPH acts as an assistance service. We are not affiliated with SM Tickets, Ticketnet, or any event organizer. Ticket availability is subject to platform and venue limitations.
