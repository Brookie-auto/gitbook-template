# 🌟 FAQ

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How does the ticket assistance work?

Simply browse available concerts and events, select the one you want, and submit a booking request. Our team will handle the ticket procurement process and keep you updated on the status.

### What platforms do you support?

We currently support ticket procurement from **SM Tickets** and **Ticketnet**, covering the majority of major concerts and events in the Philippines.

### How long does it take to secure tickets?

Processing time varies depending on the event. For most events, we confirm within **24–48 hours** of the selling date. High-demand events may take longer.

### Is my payment secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard security practices and your payment information is encrypted. You only pay once we've confirmed your tickets.

### What if the tickets are sold out?

If we're unable to secure your tickets, you'll receive a **full refund**. We'll notify you immediately and offer alternatives when available.

### How many bookings can I have at once?

You can have a maximum of **3 active bookings** at any time. Complete or cancel existing bookings to make room for new ones.

### Can I cancel a booking?

Yes, you can cancel any booking with a **PENDING** status from your My Bookings dashboard.

### How do I contact support?

Visit our [Contact Us](contact-us.md) page or reach out via email at **support@ticketsph.com**.
