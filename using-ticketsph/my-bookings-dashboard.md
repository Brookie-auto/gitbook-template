# 📊 My Bookings Dashboard

## Track Your Bookings

Your personal dashboard gives you a complete overview of all your ticket requests.

### Accessing Your Dashboard

1. Log in to your TicketsPH account
2. Click your profile or navigate to **My Bookings**

### What You'll See

Each booking card shows:

* **Event name** and thumbnail
* **Event date**
* **Ticket quantity**
* **Section preference**
* **Booking status** (Pending, Paid, or Cancelled)
* **Notes** you included with your request

### Managing Bookings

* **View details** — Click on any booking for full information
* **Cancel a booking** — Cancel a pending booking to free up a slot

{% hint style="warning" %}
Remember: you can only have **3 active bookings** at a time. If you need to book a new event, cancel or complete an existing booking first.
{% endhint %}

### Booking Slot Counter

Your dashboard displays your remaining booking slots:

```
Booking Slots: 1 of 3 remaining
```

This updates in real-time as bookings are submitted, completed, or cancelled.
