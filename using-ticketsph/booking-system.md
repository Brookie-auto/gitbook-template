# 🎫 Booking System

## How Our Booking System Works

TicketsPH uses an assisted booking model — you submit a request, and our team secures the tickets on your behalf.

### Booking Flow

```
Browse Event → Click "Secure Ticket" → Choose Section & Quantity → Submit → Team Secures Tickets → Confirmation
```

### Booking Limits

* **Maximum 3 active bookings** per user at any time
* Your remaining slots are shown on the booking page (e.g., "2 of 3 remaining")
* Complete or cancel existing bookings to free up slots

### Booking Statuses

| Status      | What It Means                                                |
| ----------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **PENDING** | Your request has been submitted and our team is working on it |
| **PAID**    | Tickets have been secured and payment is confirmed           |
| **CANCELLED** | The booking was cancelled (by you or if tickets were unavailable) |

### What Happens After You Book

1. You'll see your booking appear in **My Bookings** with a **PENDING** status
2. Our team works to secure your tickets on or around the selling date
3. Once confirmed, your status updates to **PAID**
4. If we can't secure tickets, you receive a **full refund**

{% hint style="success" %}
You only pay once we've confirmed your tickets. No confirmation = no charge.
{% endhint %}
