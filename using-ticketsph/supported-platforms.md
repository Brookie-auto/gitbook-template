# 🌐 Supported Platforms

## Where We Source Tickets

TicketsPH currently supports ticket procurement from the two major ticketing platforms in the Philippines.

### SM Tickets

The official ticketing arm of SM, covering major concerts and events at SM-affiliated venues.

* **Website:** smtickets.com
* **Venues covered:** MOA Arena, SM Megamall, and more
* **Event types:** Concerts, theater, sports, family shows

### Ticketnet

The leading ticketing platform for live entertainment in the Philippines.

* **Website:** ticketnet.com.ph
* **Venues covered:** Araneta Coliseum, Philippine Arena, and more
* **Event types:** Concerts, festivals, sports events

### How Platform Selection Works

When you browse events on TicketsPH, each event shows which platform the tickets will be sourced from. This is determined by the event organizer and venue.
